Refund & Cancellation Policy

1. Service-Based Projects

Due to the nature of custom software development and consulting services, refunds are not provided for work already completed. Payments are made based on milestones as outlined in the Service Agreement.

2. Digital Products

For pre-packaged software licenses, customers have 7 days to request a refund if the software is proven to be non-functional on their environment and technical support cannot resolve the issue.

3. Cancellation

Subscription services may be cancelled at any time through the customer portal. Access will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle.
